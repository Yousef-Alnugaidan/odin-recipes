Fried Cabbage

Easy and fast recipe

Deliciously seasoned tender fried cabbage and onions with crispy bacon is a family favourite. A quick one pot recipe suitable for any time of the year.

Best thing is it’s inexpensive and reheats perfectly in your microwave.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons grapeseed oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning, or to taste, divided
  • ½ red bell pepper, sliced
  • ½ medium green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium head cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon butter, or more to taste (Optional)
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat grapeseed oil in a large cast iron skillet over medium heat
  2. Add onions and cook until softened, about 3 minutes
  3. Add garlic, stir, and cook until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes
  4. Season with 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning and stir to combine
  5. Mix in red and green bell peppers; cook for 1 to 2 minutes
  6. Add cabbage, remaining 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning, and stir to combine
  7. Add butter and stir until melted.
  8. Simmer cabbage until it reaches desired softness, about 15 minutes
  9. Season with salt and pepper
