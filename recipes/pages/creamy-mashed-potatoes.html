Creamy mashed potatoes

This creamy mashed potatoes recipe is shockingly good

This mashed potatoes recipe has just 4 simple ingredients (one of which is salt), but there are several secrets that make this recipe work so well.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs (12 medium) russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 1/4 cups hot milk (we used whole milk)
  • 2 sticks (1 cup) unsalted butter at room temperature (not melted)
  • 1 1/2 tsp sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 Tbsp fresh parsley or chives, finely chopped for garnish

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes
  2. Drain well and transfer to the bowl of your stand mixer
  3. Grab the whisk attachment and mash potatoes lightly by hand to break them up
  4. start mixer on low speed 30 seconds then increase to medium and slowly drizzle in 1 to 1 1/4 cups HOT milk.
  5. With mixer on, add softened butter 1 Tbsp at a time, waiting a few seconds between each
  6. To keep mashed potatoes warm until serving: cover potatoes
